About Us

Crime Watch Uganda

Crime Watch Uganda is dedicated to creating peaceful, safe, and secure communities with a strong focus on crime prevention through collaborative efforts and coordinated agency action. We work closely with community members, government agencies, and various organizations to identify challenges, recommend solutions, and promote community safety and crime prevention.

Our mission involves mobilizing communities to collaborate with agencies in addressing social issues, sharing valuable information, and exchanging best practices. We empower community leaders and stakeholders by providing leadership training, equipping them to actively engage others as advocates for a safer society.
