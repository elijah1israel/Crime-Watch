Free Legal Aid and Advocacy

Our organization provides free legal aid and representation to vulnerable individuals and communities who cannot afford legal services. We believe that access to justice is a fundamental human right, and we are committed to ensuring that everyone has an equal opportunity to seek justice and protect their rights. Our team of experienced lawyers and advocates work tirelessly to provide quality legal services to our clients, and we are proud of the positive impact we have made in the lives of many people.

Juvenile Justice Advocacy

Crime Watch Uganda's Juvenile Justice Advocacy program is dedicated to promoting the rights and well-being of children and young people in conflict with the law. We work to ensure that children are treated fairly and with dignity, and that their rights are protected throughout the criminal justice process. Our advocacy programs focus on providing access to legal aid, promoting alternative sentencing options, and supporting the rehabilitation and reintegration of young people back into their communities.
