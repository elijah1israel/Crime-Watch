About Us

Crime Watch Uganda

Crime Watch Uganda is dedicated to creating peaceful, safe, and secure communities with a strong focus on crime prevention through collaborative efforts and coordinated agency action. We work closely with community members, government agencies, and various organizations to identify challenges, recommend solutions, and promote community safety and crime prevention.

Our mission involves mobilizing communities to collaborate with agencies in addressing social issues, sharing valuable information, and exchanging best practices. We empower community leaders and stakeholders by providing leadership training, equipping them to actively engage others as advocates for a safer society.

Free Legal Aid and Advocacy

Our organization provides free legal aid and representation to vulnerable individuals and communities who cannot afford legal services. We believe that access to justice is a fundamental human right, and we are committed to ensuring that everyone has an equal opportunity to seek justice and protect their rights. Our team of experienced lawyers and advocates work tirelessly to provide quality legal services to our clients, and we are proud of the positive impact we have made in the lives of many people.

Justice Advocacy

Crime Watch Uganda promotes justice and fair treatment for all individuals, through advocacy and alternative dispute resolution. We work with local authorities, community groups, and residents to identify and address the root causes of conflict, and to develop effective strategies for resolving disputes peacefully and fairly. Our advocacy programs focus on education, awareness-raising, and community engagement, and we work to empower individuals and communities to take an active role in promoting justice and resolving conflicts peacefully.

Juvenile Justice Advocacy

Crime Watch Uganda's Juvenile Justice Advocacy program is dedicated to promoting the rights and well-being of children and young people in conflict with the law. We work to ensure that children are treated fairly and with dignity, and that their rights are protected throughout the criminal justice process. Our advocacy programs focus on providing access to legal aid, promoting alternative sentencing options, and supporting the rehabilitation and reintegration of young people back into their communities.
